About Me [ CV ]

I am currently an M.Sc. student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University, supervised by Prof. Limin Wang. Previously, I received the B.Sc. degree from Kuang Yaming Honors School, Nanjing University in 2020 (overall gpa 4.44/5, rank 3/29).
My primary research focus on video understanding and sparse, query-based detection of events and activities in temporal aspect. In the future, I hope to build perception systems for general visual understanding with minimal human supervision.
